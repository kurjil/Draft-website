About ANATKYA INVESTMENT

Building Juba's Future, One Material at a Time

Our Story

ANATKYA INVESTMENT is a trusted construction materials supplier serving Juba and the surrounding regions. With years of experience in the industry, we have built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality building materials, reliable service, and competitive pricing to contractors, developers, and individual builders. Based in Juba, we specialize in supplying durable and cost-effective materials, including concrete hollow blocks designed to meet the demands of local construction projects. We understand the unique building conditions in South Sudan, which allows us to provide practical solutions that ensure strength, safety, and long-lasting results. At ANATKYA INVESTMENT, customer satisfaction is at the core of everything we do. From timely deliveries to consistent product quality, we are committed to supporting projects of all sizes across Juba and nearby areas. Whether you are working on residential, commercial, or large-scale developments, we aim to be your reliable partner for all construction material needs.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality construction materials that meet international standards while keeping them affordable for the local market. We believe in reliability, transparency, and customer satisfaction in every transaction.
